# 🎯 Research — Problem Statement & HMW

Synthesize research findings into a clear problem statement and generate actionable "How Might We" (HMW) questions to guide design solutions.

## The Formula

**Problem Statement** = Research + Insight + Design Direction

Example:
```
Research Finding:
"Users spend 5+ minutes finding the upload button in settings"

Insight:
"The upload feature is buried and not discoverable"

Problem Statement:
"We need to make photo upload more discoverable so users can update their profile photo in < 30 seconds"

Design Hypothesis:
"If we surface the photo upload feature prominently in the profile header, users will complete uploads faster and feel more in control"
```

## How Might We (HMW) Questions

Generate 5-10 HMW questions from your problem statement. These guide ideation.

Example HMWs:
```
- How might we make photo upload visible without cluttering the profile header?
- How might we provide feedback during upload so users know it's working?
- How might we let users retry if upload fails?
- How might we let users crop/adjust photos before upload?
- How might we show upload history so users can change their photo later?
```

## Output Deliverable

A Problem Statement Document:
1. **Research Findings**: 3-5 key findings from research
2. **Insights**: What do these findings mean?
3. **Problem Statement**: Clear, actionable problem
4. **Design Hypothesis**: "If we...then users will..."
5. **Success Criteria**: How will we know we solved it?
6. **HMW Questions**: 5-10 questions to guide design
7. **Initial Ideas**: 2-3 design directions to explore

This becomes the brief for the design team.
